How I accidentally became the Tech Guy 👉👈

I accidentally wandered into the world of tech the way people accidentally open 47 tabs — one thing led to another and suddenly everything was broken. It started with a tiny program that refused to work, and instead of doing the sensible thing (closing my laptop and eating snacks), I decided to “fix” it… which somehow created five new problems. After countless tutorials, copy-paste disasters, and questioning all my life choices, the code finally worked — and I got hooked. Now I’m the kind of person who proudly says, “It works… don’t touch it,” while secretly hoping it doesn’t break again.
